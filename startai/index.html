About StartAI
StartAI is a conference for undergraduate students in Ontario, aimed at increasing their involvement in the growing Canadian Artificial Intelligence ecosystem. The University of Toronto is home to world class Artificial Intelligence research, which is one of the reasons that it attracts some of the best and brightest undergraduate students to the city.

StartAI provides an opportunity to leverage this reputation to create further student engagement, resulting in long term benefits for Canadian work in Artificial Intelligence.

Our aim is to improve the communication and availability of opportunities in the Canadian Artificial Intelligence space to undergraduate students in a manner that develops a sustainable pipeline from education to work and research in the field.
